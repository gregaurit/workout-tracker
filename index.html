How it works

This app stores data in your browser using local storage. That means your entries stay on this device and browser unless you export them.

  • Today’s date is loaded automatically.
  • You can still change the date if you’re entering a past workout.
  • Each saved workout writes one record per set.
  • CSV export gives you a spreadsheet-ready backup.
